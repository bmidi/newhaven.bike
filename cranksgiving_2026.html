Cranksgiving 2026

Saturday, November 23, 2026

Cranksgiving is a charity bike ride and food drive. Bring a bicycle, a lock, and a bag.

Please arrive at the New Haven Green flagpole by XX:XX AM. We will leave together at XX:XX AM and plan to return to DESK by X:XX PM, where volunteers will be on hand until X:XX PM.
